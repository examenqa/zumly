# Visual Customization

Zumly gives you full control over how your recording looks — from the background and device frame around the video to the effects shown when you click.

---

## Backgrounds

The background fills the space around your recorded content. Choose from **84 presets** organized into three categories:

| Category | Count | Description |
| -------- | ----- | ----------- |
| **Solid** | 39 | Flat fills — from soft lights (Pure White, Light Blue) to rich darks (Dark Purple, Blue Black) |
| **Gradient** | 37 | Smooth color blends: 20 linear (top-to-bottom), 11 radial (glowing center), 6 spotlight (off-center glow) |
| **Pattern** | 8 | Organic wavy designs layered over a gradient base |

Use the category dropdown in the **BACKGROUND** section to switch between groups. Click any swatch to apply it — the preview updates immediately.

---

## Device Frames

Device frames add a border around your recorded video to make it look like it's displayed on a screen or device. Choose from **5 presets** in the **DEVICE FRAME** section:

| Frame | Look |
| ----- | ---- |
| **Wide Bezel** | Thick dark border with a camera dot at the top — classic monitor feel |
| **Slim Bezel** | Thinner dark border with a camera dot — a more modern look |
| **Thin Border** | Minimal dark edge — clean and understated |
| **Shadow Only** | No border, just a soft floating shadow — the video appears to float |
| **No Frame** | Edge-to-edge video — pure, no decoration |

---

## Output Size

Control the shape of the exported video with the **Output Size** selector:

| Preset | Dimensions | Best for |
| ------ | ---------- | -------- |
| **Auto (source)** | Matches your recording | Default — no cropping or padding |
| **16:9** | 1920 × 1080 | Standard widescreen, YouTube |
| **3:2** | 1620 × 1080 | Tablets and some laptops |
| **4:3** | 1440 × 1080 | Classic presentation format |
| **1:1** | 1080 × 1080 | Social media squares (Instagram) |
| **9:16** | 1080 × 1920 | Vertical video (TikTok, Reels, Shorts) |

---

## Click Effects

Every mouse click in your recording can be shown as an animated visual effect, making it obvious to viewers exactly where and when you clicked. Choose from **8 presets** in the **CLICK EFFECTS** section:

| Preset | Style |
| ------ | ----- |
| **Subtle Purple** | Expanding purple ripple circles (default) |
| **Bold Red** | High-visibility red ripple |
| **Neon Cyan** | Bright cyan expanding circles |
| **Minimal Gray** | Understated gray ripple |
| **High Contrast Yellow** | Maximum visibility yellow |
| **Clean White** | Clean white expanding circles |
| **Soft Green** | Gentle green ripple |
| **Invisible** | No click effect — clicks are not shown |

!!! tip "Cleaning up accidental clicks"
    You can select individual click events on the timeline's **Click track** and delete them before export. This lets you keep a clean click effect preset while removing any unwanted markers.

---

## Cursor

Your mouse cursor is automatically rendered into the exported video at the recorded position — you don't need to configure anything. It appears as an arrow cursor scaled to match the output resolution.
